Software House

Terragon's Software House is responsible for the support, maintenance, and research and development of the Terragon RHM. In addition to its primary focus on ensuring the stability and continuous delivery of the product roadmap, the Software House also oversees other internal projects and brands.

Through our tailored approach, we provide bespoke recommendations and assemble specialized teams that are dedicated to each partner. Each partner is assigned a dedicated Account Manager and has direct access to a team of engineers within their assigned pod, ensuring a highly collaborative and responsive support structure.
